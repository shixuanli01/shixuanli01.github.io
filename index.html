Shixuan Li (李诗轩)

Hi everyone! 👋 I am a 3rd-year Ph.D. student in Computer Engineering at the University of Southern California, advised by Prof. Paul Bogdan. I received my M.S. in Computer Engineering from USC (2025) and my B.E. in Computer Science from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (2023).

My research interests focus on Large Language Models (LLMs) and Multi-Agent Systems, including reasoning and collaboration mechanisms. I also work on neural operators, generative models, and their applications in domains such as hardware design (HDL) and weather forecasting.
